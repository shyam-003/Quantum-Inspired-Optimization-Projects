
Problem
-----------
Implement a VQE algorithm to find the ground state energy of simple molecule like H_2 or LiH


Workflow
-----------

1. Define your molecule
        A. Define your molecule ( here H2 molecule)
        B. Define geometry ( here define the distance between the molecule)
        C. Define Hamiltonian ( Use qiskit / pennylane to generate the electronic hamiltonian of a molecule)

2. Map the Problem to Qubits
        A. Mapping : Apply Jordan-wigner or Bravyi-Kitaev transformation
        B. Result : convert fermionic hamiltonian to qubit hamiltonian

3. Design a Quantum circuit
        A. Parameters
        B. Common choice for circuit : UCCSD

4. Choose classical optimizer
